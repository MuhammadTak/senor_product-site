privacy & personalization

Build your
own model.

Every time you use senor, it learns a bit more about how you work — your domain, your style, the kind of answers that actually help you. The more you use it, the better it gets at working with you specifically.

That learning is entirely yours. Nothing you send to senor ever influences anyone else's model, and nobody else's usage can shape yours. You're not feeding a shared system — you're developing something personal.

The foundation everyone starts from stays untouched. What grows over time is your layer on top of it, one that reflects exactly how you work.

Want to pause the learning?

You can turn it off anytime from your account settings. Your model keeps everything it's already learned about you — it just won't pick up anything new until you re-enable it. Nothing is lost, nothing resets. It simply stops growing.
