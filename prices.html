faq

Common questions.

Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?

Yes. Plan changes take effect immediately. If you downgrade mid-cycle, you keep the higher tier's limits until the period ends and aren't charged the difference.

What happens when I hit the free tier request limit?

Requests are paused until midnight UTC. You'll get a clear error response with a retry_after header. No surprise charges, no silent failures.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes — 14 days of Pro access, no credit card required. When the trial ends you're automatically moved to the Free plan unless you add a payment method.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes. Annual plans are billed at 10 months instead of 12 — roughly a 17% discount. Switch anytime from your billing settings.

Is there an open-source or student discount?

Yes. We offer free Pro access for qualifying open-source maintainers and students. Apply here with a link to your project or institution.
